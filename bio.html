Systematic Age

Urban Ukrainian poetry

Ternopil, Ukraine, the end of the 90s. The family that lives on the suburbs of the regional center: a father, a mother and a little daughter. On one of April nights, after Dynamo Kyiv sensational campaign in the Champions League, he was born. A boy that is the central figure in this story.


Systematic Age (as a real person - Yurij Lishchuk) spent all his lifetime in his hometown - Ternopil, where he was fulfilled by spirits of urban atmosphere of Ternopil panel houses.


Yurij has studied eleven years at the school №29 where he not just get a lot of knowledge from teachers but he also began to write poems. At first, he was not so good at them but shortly after he made a breakthrough in his poetry skills.


Themes of his poets are very different: from love to war, from history to inner, from pain to power, etc. In general, Systematic Age's poems contain not only expressions, feelings, anger but also valuable facts that maybe nobody knew about before.
